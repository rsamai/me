About

You can find me hacking, snacking, or napping.

I’m a passionate leader and 3rd Year Computer Science student at Cal Poly SLO. I empower people to make the world a better place through hackathons and personal ventures. I focus on user experience, product management, and human-centered design.

One of my core priorities is increasing diversity and communication about technology. I do this through hosting inclusive events and talks on The Heroine’s Journey. Catch me speaking at Girls Who Code program campuses(Amazon, eBay) and online to join my journey in waving the pink flag.
